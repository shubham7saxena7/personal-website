Creating What I Consume

For a long time, I have been a passive consumer of entertainment, food, and whatnot. I would spend hours watching TV shows, scrolling through social media, and ordering takeout. I enjoyed the convenience and instant gratification of having everything at my fingertips, and I also rationalised it to myself by saying, “This is the convenience money buys”. Recently, I have started to feel a sense of emptiness and disconnection from my creativity and passions.
